Grand City History

Grand City was created by an ambitious investor named Tjung Sun Liong
on 12th December 2012, while its grand opening falls on 19th November 2021.
The Grand mall *pun intended*, are a spacious one with approximately
250,000 sqm land areas and a hefty 10 stories high to its name. Created with
the help from a world-known architect and business consultant, Wirawan
Ekaputra. Despite has only been open to public in the last year, Grand
City as we say right now is a top choice among the public, and also the critics.
Since its opening, Grand city has been receiving award left and right in many
categories, one of which is ' The Future Mall ' by Brew & On The Hill.co.
Or a more reknown one such as, the winning of The Most Enchanting &
Captivating Design Award by Europe Art & Property Design in January 2022.
Even though these awards and achievements are sure a fool-proof way to mark
progress, the Grand City Head Department has never stop to try and always
giving you the best that they could. They take pride in what they do, and had
never fail in Striving To Be The Best. They will always try, implement, and evolve,
with more impressive, authenthic and original idea to serve the loyal visitors.
